About Nagaraj Footwears

Quality Footwear Crafted With Experience

Nagaraj Footwears has been serving customers with quality footwear, leather craftsmanship, and footwear repair services. We specialize in creating customized shoes and slippers for physically disabled individuals, ensuring comfort, durability, and the perfect fit.

Our shop also offers premium leather shoes, leather slippers, retail footwear for men, women, and children, along with expert shoe repair and stitching services.
